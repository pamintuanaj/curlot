Our Story

Curlot was born out of my personal journey of self-acceptance and empowerment. Growing up with curly hair, I struggled to love my natural beauty. For years, I tried to conform to societal standards with harsh chemicals and treatments. But during the pandemic, I reflected on my values and priorities. I let my hair grow, and eventually, I cut off my rebonded hair to let my natural curls shine. This journey inspired me to create a platform where others with curly hair could find inspiration, education, and community. I wanted to create a space where people could celebrate their unique beauty, free from societal pressure.
